Cartisan offers a host of services at car owner's doorstep including services like Car wash and Detailed cleaning, Minor repairs, Periodic maintenance service for oil-change, Breakdown assistance, etc
For doorstep services, Cartisan will arrange a team of technicians/cleaning crew who will bring all necessary items, tools, equipment with no specific items required from the car owner
Car owner will need to ensure permissions allowed for the Cartisan team to enter into the residential complex for access to the car, and ensure no objections from neighbours, apartment association, security guards, etc while the Cartisan crew is working on the car. Also, it is important the car owner ensures no valuables are left inside the car during the time service is being delivered on the car.
