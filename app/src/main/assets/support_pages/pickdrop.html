Callsikandar offers pickup and drop option for car services that need to be performed at Callsikandar preferred service centres. Callsikandar personnel will come to your location and pickup the vehicle for servicing and drop back the vehicle post completion of service.
During the service and post completion of service, the car owner will be kept informed via phone, messages, pictures and videos on the progress of service.
During pickup from car owner's premises and after drop of the car back to car owner, Cartisan personnel will provide inspection checklist report of the car.
