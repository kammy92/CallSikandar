DEMOCRACY:

According to the Oxford dictionary, "democracy" is the practice or principles of social equiality. The origin is from ancient Greece: demokratia, from demos 'the people' + kratia 'power, rule." In essence it is the power of the people.

VOTESOCRACY:

Is a public opinion platform using your individual votes, ratings, rankings and surveys to gauge how you really feel.

TOPICS INCLUDED:

Consumer retail, entertainment, health sports, science, environment, business, technology, news, politics, religion and even sex.

HOW?:

There is a possibility that when you see "10,000 likes" on a website, that these 10,000 likes were not really generated by 10,000 humans. In fact, they could have been created by "bots." Internet robots, or programs created to overstate the likes. We created a program using simple voice recognition technology that already exists to confirm that you are really a human and not an internet robot.

VOTING

This includes Polls, Petitions, and even Consumer Complaints. You can vote on any subject, you can create a petition and send it to a government official or company leader, and even socialize a consumer complaint to see if others are going through the same pain you are experiencing.

RATING

This includes rating a location such as a hotel or restaurant, you can rate photos, and or rate anything else you want.

RANKING

Add any number of entries and socialize this to see what your friends or customers think of something. We use the standard "Borda" method to calculate the ratings.

SURVEY

You can add the VOTE + RATE + RANK features and create a customized survey. Individuals or Companies can use this to understand many things about any subject or even about you.

GETTING PAID FOR YOUR VOTES!

If an individual or company decides to pay for these features, you will get paid. You can even search our database by how much each feature will pay.

RESULTS

There are 2 types of results. Global results and local results. Certain polls and surveys may be limited to geographical locations based on mobile phone location services. For example, if the question is "Who do you want to be the next president of the United States?" then the local results will reflect user votes based in the USA. However, if you choose Global results, you will see what the rest of the world thinks of this question as well.

LEVELS

Users can be individuals or an individual representing a company or organization.

Level 1:

As a LEVEL 1 user, all you have to enter is your email ID. This will allow you to search and browse all the results but your results will only be counted in the Global results. You can never participate in local based polls or surveys and can never get paid.

Level 2:

As a LEVEL 2 user and in addition to Level 1 requirements, you must enter your first and last name, you date of birth, gender and get voice recognition validation. This will allow you to VOTE.

Level 3:

As a LEVEL 3 user, you will enter personal information about yourself in order to get PAID for polls and surveys. There is a special feature where the more information you provide, the more you will get paid for each survey. We think you will agree, that why should companies take this information from you, when you have the choice to sell it?
